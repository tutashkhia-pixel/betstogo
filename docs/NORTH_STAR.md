# North Star

BETS→GO does not predict the future.

BETS→GO continuously evaluates the quality of every available decision as new information arrives.

The goal is not to predict winners. The goal is to consistently make better decisions than the market participant would make without BETS→GO.

Every feature, every algorithm, every screen, every recommendation and every line of code must support this mission.

## Design principles

1. **Decision Quality > Outcome.** A good decision can lose. A bad decision can win. BETS→GO optimizes decision quality, not hindsight.
2. **Dollars before probabilities.** Recommendations are communicated in actual dollars wherever possible — "here is exactly how your financial outcome changes under every possible result."
3. **Explain every recommendation.** Never ask the user to trust the AI. Every recommendation states what changed, why it matters, how confident the system is, the exact financial consequences, and what information would improve confidence.
4. **No forced decisions.** "No Action" is a valid recommendation. Sometimes preserving capital is the highest-EV decision.
5. **Risk is always visible.** Every screen shows current exposure, maximum loss, lockable profit, expected value, and the decision score.
6. **Every recommendation is ranked.** The Action Board ranks every available action: 🥇 best expected value · 🛡 best capital protection · ⚖ best risk-adjusted.
7. **Event driven.** Major events (goals, red cards, odds swings, cash-out changes, halftime, 70', 80', 85', 88') automatically trigger a pulse check.
8. **Build for traders, not gamblers.** The interface is professional trading software; every recommendation resembles an investment decision, not a betting tip.
9. **Human + AI partnership.** Live market pricing, match analytics, and operator observations combine; no single source dominates.
10. **Continuous learning.** Every completed trade is journaled — every pulse, recommendation, action, outcome, and lesson.

## Current mission scope (experiment phase)

BETS→GO is an experimental decision-support platform. The goal is not to build a company, a business, or support for every sport. The goal is to determine whether BETS→GO **measurably improves live sports trading decisions**. Features that do not help validate that hypothesis are deferred. Build for learning before building for scale.
