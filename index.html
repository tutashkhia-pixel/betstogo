Model & method.

Every number BETS→GO shows is computed from these rules — nothing is a black box. Under the hood the core engines are market-agnostic and produce facts only; a single Decision Orchestrator turns facts into advice. This page documents the Football market adapter, the first supported market.

1 · In-play goal model

Remaining goals for each side follow a Poisson process. Base rate is your pre-match xG scaled by time remaining, with a late-game uplift (second halves produce ~55% of goals):

λ = xG × (90 − min)/90 × (1 + 0.20 × min/90)

  • Red cards: the short-handed side's rate ×0.72 per card; opponent ×1.18.
  • Momentum: each slider step shifts rates ±9% between the sides.
  • Game state: after 55', a trailing side pushes (+6% per goal down, max 2); the leader manages (−5%).

The joint distribution of additional goals (0–10 each side) prices every derivative market: 1X2, totals, team totals, handicaps, BTTS, next goal. "Next goal" assumes the next scorer splits by rate share: P = λ_team/(λ_h+λ_a) × P(≥1 more goal).

2 · Edge & EV

American odds convert to implied probability (+120 → 45.5%, −150 → 60%). Edge = model probability − implied probability. EV per $1 = p×(dec−1) − (1−p). Implied probabilities include the book's margin, so a small positive edge may just be vig — BETS→GO flags edges under 2 points as noise.

3 · Trade Score & confidence

Trade Score = 50 + edge(pts) × 3.2, clamped 0–100. 50 = fairly priced. Confidence blends edge size, match progress (later = more certain), and input completeness.

4 · Cash-Out Engine

Expected Hold Value = P(bet wins) × total payout. The offer is judged by ratio = offer / EHV: ≥1.05 full cash out · 0.97–1.05 partial · 0.88–0.97 wait · <0.88 hold. Books price cash-outs below fair value on purpose; taking a sub-0.9 ratio repeatedly is a slow leak.

5 · Hedge Engine

Active only while the original bet retains realistic winning probability (25–80%). Equal-profit stake: hedge = payout / dec_hedge. BETS→GO shows net in every scenario plus the worst case; it recommends a hedge only when it locks value, not to soothe nerves.

6 · Recovery Opportunity Engine

Activates only when the original position is effectively dead (win probability < 25%). Every live market is priced independently: model probability vs. implied. Positive-EV markets are ranked; suggested stake is quarter-Kelly, capped at 50% of the original stake. If nothing clears the bar the verdict is NO RECOVERY TRADE — being down is never a reason to bet.

7 · Action Board

Every available option — hold, full/partial cash-out, hedge, best recovery trade — is priced as net P/L in dollars: Expected (probability-weighted), Worst case, Best case. Ranks: 🥇 highest expected value · 🛡 highest worst-case (capital protection) · ⚖ best risk-adjusted (expected − 0.4 × (expected − worst)). "Hold / No action" is always on the board — preserving capital is a position.

8 · Decision Quality Score

0–100, scored at close: positive-edge entry (20) · stake ≤ 5% of bankroll (15) · followed the cash-out engine (20) · no negative-EV recovery placed (20) · no chasing / emotional adds (15) · review completed (10). DQS is the number to maximize over a season.

Honest limits

This MVP prices from your manual inputs and pre-match xG estimates — it has no injury feeds, lineups, sharp-money data, or live stats. Treat outputs as a disciplined second opinion, not ground truth.
